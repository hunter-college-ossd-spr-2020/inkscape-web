The Inkscape Fund is a way to show appreciation to Inkscape developers.

Your donations are used for supporting Inkscape in various ways, most particularly to help cover the costs of organising hackfests and gathering Inkscape developers at important conferences such as Libre Graphics Convention and SCALE. These are events that developers would not otherwise be able to attend.

How do I donate?

The Inkscape Fund is currently managed by the Software Freedom Conservancy,, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. All donations made through it are tax-deductible in the USA to the extent permitted by law. If you would like to contribute to the Inkscape project, please use one of these payment methods:
