Help make Inkscape better

Participate in our thriving community! There are lots of ways to contribute to the Inkscape project. You don't need to be a programming whiz to get involved.

Spread the word

Think Inkscape is awesome? So do we! But there are still lots of people who don't know about it. Help us promote Inkscape and get it into their hot little hands.

Help others

Inkscape relies on volunteers for technical support. Share your know-how in the Q&A section, the forum, the IRC channel and the mailing list.

Test & report bugs

Testing and writing detailed bug reports is a very effective way to help improve Inkscape. Blah blah yadda yadda.

Write

If you're deft at writing, consider joining the Inkscape Manual team, editing and updating the User Documentation, or writing some killer tutorials.

Translate

Inkscape has multilingual support. Join the translation team to help translate Inkscape's interface, website pages, documentation and tutorials.

Code & develop

Developers can help by submitting patches, writing extensions, and blah blah yadda yadda.

Make art

New users want to see what Inkscape is capable of. Show off your talent by submitting your art work to the gallery. You can also contribute to icon themes.

Provide feedback

Have an idea for how we can make Inkscape better? Make a feature request, add a testimonial, or have a chat with developers on the mailing list.

Donate

You can show your appreciation of Inkscape with tax-deductible donations of any amount. See how we use your financial contributions.
